# AWAKENED
## Book One Outline

---

### SERIES PREMISE
A graphic designer discovers that reality is a simulation after trauma triggers her ability to see "the code" behind existence. As she levels up and gains the power to manipulate reality, she accidentally kills a man and discovers that she's not the first to awaken - beneath the surface code lies the Sediment: layers upon layers of buried reality, traces of awakened people throughout history, and something ancient waiting at the foundation. With her found family of fellow awakened, she must dig deeper to understand what she's become - even as something watches from below.

---

### MAIN CHARACTERS

**Samantha Marion** — 30, graphic designer, Slytherin to the core. Dry, sardonic, overthinks everything. Pop culture brain (romantasy audiobooks, sci-fi references). Was a workaholic who poured herself into her career, leaving her with a nice apartment she barely lives in and almost no personal connections. Self-preservation is her default, but she's not heartless—just practical about survival. Has a complicated past with her college roommate Sarah that she's never fully processed.

**Scott Mitchell** — FBI/Financial crimes investigator, ethical hacker. Handsome, nerdy-funny, genuinely likeable. Goes undercover to catch Samantha for bank fraud but develops real feelings. Chooses Sam over his career, but his journal is stolen before he can warn her. Witnesses the aftermath and his guilt triggers his own awakening.

**Kate Frank** — Samantha's one real friend, still works at the company. Loyal at first, helps investigate the framing, but pulls away when Samantha's reckless sabotage threatens everything. Represents what Samantha loses by embracing her new reality.

**Daniel Park** — The direct report who framed Samantha. Was stealing her designs and selling them to Vertex Communications. Slimy corporate climber who got promoted after Sam's firing.

**Sarah** — Sam's college roommate. They were extremely close, possibly toed a romantic line, but Sam pulled away and chose work over the relationship. Now estranged, exchanging only holiday pleasantries. Sam regrets how she handled it.

**Christopher Dyer** — Scott's FBI supervisor. Relentless investigator who steals the journal from Scott's hotel room. Leads the arrest of Sam, but is humiliated when all evidence of the journal's contents disappears. Placed on administrative leave, but he knows what he saw. Will return.

**Officer James Reilly** — The officer who dies during Sam's arrest. 34, married, two kids. Sam accidentally destabilizes his brain pattern when he grabs her arm roughly. His death haunts Sam throughout the series.

---

### BOOK ONE STRUCTURE

**Target Length:** ~130,000+ words (14 chapters + epilogue)
**Tone:** Sci-fi humor (Hitchhiker's Guide / Dungeon Crawler Carl) with thriller elements and genuine emotional stakes

### CHAPTER 1: "The Static" (COMPLETE)
**~8,000 words**

**Opening:** Samantha's morning routine. Broken coffee maker (E7 error). Establishing her as a successful but hollow workaholic. Nice apartment that looks like a furniture showroom—nothing personal. Romantasy audiobook on the commute (faerie king betrayal).

**The Setup:** At work, tension with Daniel (her direct report) has been building. CC'ing higher-ups, acting smug. Meridian project due in two days.

**The Hammer Falls:** Called into HR. Greg and Rebecca. The accusation: Vertex Communications announced branding yesterday that matches her Meridian designs. Daniel "noticed" and reported it to "prevent an incident."

**The Awakening:** As they talk, Sam's brain short-circuits. Ringing in ears. The static on the wall organizes into patterns. **LEVEL UP.** Nosebleed. Escorted out by security (Marcus). Brief moment with Kate in the hallway.

**The Aftermath:** Drives home. Migraine pills. Tries to see static again, fails. Sees shimmer on her hands in peripheral vision. Googles symptoms, goes to ER at 4 AM. CT scan, MRI, diagnosed with "complex migraine." Buys carbon monoxide detector.

**Mom Call:** Woken by mom calling about Thanksgiving. Pressure about bringing a boyfriend, whispered concern about being a "lesbian." Sam is furious.

**The Breakthrough:** In the shower, distracted by mom stress, the static comes faster. After eating a questionable frozen turkey dinner, she pushes into the code and violently vomits. But when she recovers, her old cream blanket is now brown. **LEVEL UP.**

**Kate Visit:** Kate shows up Monday demanding answers. Notices the walls are now ice blue. Sam deflects, doesn't reveal abilities. Kate promises to help build a case against Daniel. Pizza. Friendship moment.

**Closing:** Sam alone, watching sunset. The code is visible now without trying. Daniel has no idea what's coming.
